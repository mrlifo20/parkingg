General Questions

PARKSYNC is a smart urban parking platform that helps you find, book, and manage parking spaces effortlessly. We connect drivers with available parking spots in real-time, making city parking stress-free.

Simply search for parking near your destination, compare prices and amenities, book your spot, and use the QR code for seamless check-in. It's that easy!

We're expanding rapidly! PARKSYNC is currently available in major metropolitan areas across the US, Canada, and Europe. Check our search page to see available parking near you.

Booking Questions

Search for parking, select your preferred spot, choose your duration, enter your vehicle details, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a QR code for check-in.

Yes! You can extend your booking directly from your reservations page, subject to availability. We'll send you a reminder before your booking expires.

You can cancel your booking up to 1 hour before the scheduled start time for a full refund. Cancellations within 1 hour may be subject to a 50% fee.

Payment Questions

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Yes, absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and partner with Stripe for secure payment processing. We never store your complete card details.

Account Questions

Click the "Sign Up" button, fill in your details, and verify your email. You can also sign up using Google or Facebook for quicker registration.

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and we'll send you a reset link. The link expires in 24 hours.

For Parking Space Owners

Register as a parking space owner, fill in your space details, upload photos, set your pricing, and submit for verification. Once approved, your space will be live!

PARKSYNC charges a small commission on successful bookings. There are no upfront listing fees. Contact us for detailed pricing information.
